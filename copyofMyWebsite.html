I am a current senior in high school interested in Computer Science, and social justice and diversity. I have participated in several programs introducing girls to the STEAM fields and computer science. I am deeply passionate about teaching girls to code and increasing the number of women in science and technology fields. I have participated in summer programs such as Artemis at Brown University, Spira at Brown University, Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Twitter, and I have worked as a Teaching Assistant for Girls Who Code at Microsoft. To further my interests in diversity and inclusion, especially for students of color in independent schools, I have participated in Association of Independent Schools of New England (AISNE) National Association of Independent Schools (NAIS) People of Color Conferences, Student Diversty and Leadership Conferences (SDLC), and have co-founded a Cultural Inclusion Committee at my school involving faculty and students.
